Welcome

This is a Flood Prediction System. Our system allows you to predict flood, rainfall(mm), snowfall, vegetation, temperature(C).

Steps:

  1. 1. To get started, click "Get Started" on the homepage.
  2. 2. Enter the year of the future.
  3. 3. Select the month of corresponding year.
  4. 4. Select the Province.
  5. 5. Press the "Predict" button.









Result :

  1. 1. You will be provided with the result page.
  2. 2. You will be provided with the prediction of flood, rainfall(mm), snowfall, vegetation, temperature(C).
